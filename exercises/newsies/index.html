Hyper-Intelligent Pigs Build Rocket

By Oinkton Scree

Many have criticized the appearance of random bits of metal junk around the McPorker ranch for the last several months, but inside sources say there has been a method to the apparent madness. Just last week a bizarre scaffolding seemed to rise out of the muddy area behind the corn

silo and people have been asking questions. According to Chunk Nugget, a pig from the McPorker farm, the pigs have been slowing accumulating the necessary materials and fuel to build and power an innaugural flight into the planet's orbit. "It should really only take about 16 more pounds of Farmer McPorker's refuse." When asked if the pig-engineers understood that in order to go into space and begin orbitting the planet, a vessel must actually clear the Earth's atmosphere, Nugget seemed to suddenly no longer speak English. Farmer McPorker could not be reached for comment. ~
